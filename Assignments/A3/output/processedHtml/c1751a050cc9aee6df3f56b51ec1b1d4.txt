The Nigeria Football Federation (NFF) and NIKE who are their official kits sponsor have unveiled the official 2018 World Cup kits for the Super Eagles.
Many have been waiting since last year for the Nigeria Football Federation to show the world the kits the Super Eagles will use in Russia, and the unveiling has been done in London on Wednesday, February 7. The home kit carries a familiar badge recolored to honor the 96 gold medal-winning Dream Team.
Super Eagles of Nigeria are in Group D for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia where they will face bitter rivals Argentina, Croatia and Iceland.
