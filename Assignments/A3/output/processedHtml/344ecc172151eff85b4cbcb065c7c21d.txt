Tottenham 2-0 Newport: Erik Lamela nets first goal in 504 days after Dan Butler's own goal as Mauricio Pochettino's side avoid upset to reach fifth round
Mauricio Pochettino's Spurs earned their place in the fifth round with a 2-0 victory on Wednesday night
Tottenham took the lead after Moussa Sissoko's low cross was deflected into his own net by Dan Butler
Erik Lamela doubled the home side's advantage with his first goal for the club since September 2016 
Awaiting the victorious Spurs in the next round is a trip to League One strugglers Rochdale on February 18
