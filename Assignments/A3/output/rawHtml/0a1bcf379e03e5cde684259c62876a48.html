SEMO football \x97\xa0National Signing Day 2018

\n
Posted Wednesday, February 7, 2018, at 1:10 PM
\n
\n
Southeast Missouri State football coach Tom Matukewicz talks to his players during a practice during the 2017 season. On Wednesday, the Redhawks announced a 2018 signing class of 22 new players.
\n
\r\n

Southeast Missouri State football National Signing Day press conference - coach Tom Matukewicz https://t.co/aNSogxxBMl

\r\n— semoball.com (@semoball) February 7, 2018
\r\n\r\n\r\n
\r\n

Read capsules on all 22 of SEMO's 2018 recruiting class HERE.

\r\n\r\n

What are the key talking points for the incoming group? Here are some things to know: Santa is coming, early signing effect and local Redhawks

\r\n\r\n
\r\n

Once again it's the first Wednesday in February, which means it's National Signing Day.

\r\n\r\n

This year is a little different from the past, in that an early signing window in late December gave college recruits an opportunity to sign before today, thinning out the action a bit on what has become a sort of national holiday for diehard college sports fans.

\r\n\r\n

Southeast Missouri State football announced Wednesday a signing class of 22 total players, 13 of which were inked back in December during the early window. That left nine to join the fold today.

\r\n\r\n

Look for more on the signing class later.

\r\n\r\n

The positional breakdown is as follows:

\r\n\r\n

QB: 3
\r\nRB: 3
\r\nWR: 2
\r\nTE: 1
\r\nOL: 4
\r\nDL: 2
\r\nLB: 2
\r\nDB: 5

\r\n\r\n

The geographic breakdown is as follows:

\r\n\r\n

Mississippi: 5
\r\nIllinois: 4
\r\nMissouri: 3
\r\nTennessee: 3
\r\nGeorgia: 3
\r\nIowa: 1
\r\nTexas: 1
\r\nOklahoma: 1
\r\nNevada: 1

\r\n\r\n

Today's group includes:

\r\n\r\n

Cameron John (Brighton, Tenn.) — OLB, 6-4, 215 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Zion Custis (Lovejoy, Ga.) — RB, 5-8, 180 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Aaron Alston (Brighton, Tenn.) — WR, 6-4, 185 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Nate Korte (Morris, Ill.) — OL, 6-6, 295 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Trai Hodges (Rome, Ga.) — DB, 6-0, 175 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Josh Price (Vicksburg, Miss./Hinds CC) — DL, 6-2, 295 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Cameron Meeks (Austin, Texas/Butler CC) — DL, 6-2, 295 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Mark Robinson (Leesburg, Ga./Presbyterian College) — RB, 6-0, 215 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Keshon Heard (Hamilton, Miss./Itawamba CC) — DB, 5-11, 175 pounds

\r\n\r\n

The early signing group includes:

\r\n\r\n

Warren Byrd (Tunica, Miss.) — DB, 6-1, 190 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Caleb Strauss (Warrenton, Mo.) — TE, 6-8, 230 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Will Christopher (Girard, Ill.) — OL, 6-5, 290 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Lawrence Johnson (St. Louis, Mo.) — DB, 6-1, 170 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Daniel Santacaterina (Geneva, Ill/Northern Illinois) — QB, 6-2, 205

\r\n\r\n

Jeremy Tucker (Charleston, Mo.) — LB, 6-2, 220 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Geno Hess (Peoria, Ill.) — RB, 5-9, 185 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Preston Haynes (Olive Branch, Miss./Itawamba CC) — OL, 6-5, 305 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Eli Swehla (Des Moines, Iowa/Iowa Western CC) — OL, 6-3, 300 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Jacob Buie (Memphis, Tenn.) — QB, 6-5, 200 pounds

\r\n\r\n

DJ Freeman (Del City, Okla./Northeastern Oklahoma A&M) — DB, 6-2, 210 pounds

\r\n\r\n

Jerrick Orr (Aberdeen, Miss./Northwest Mississippi CC) — WR, 6-0, 195 pounds

Joe Pyle (Fallon, Nev./Golden West College) — QB, 6-2, 225 pounds
