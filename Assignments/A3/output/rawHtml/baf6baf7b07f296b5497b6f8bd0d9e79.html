\n \n Description\n

\n \n \n \n \n

A narrative show about football and the human connection behind the beautiful game. Featuring interesting and insightful short stories, from personal narratives to club histories and everything in between. By Association is an award-winning podcast, hosted and produced by James Parkinson. Episodes released monthly.
