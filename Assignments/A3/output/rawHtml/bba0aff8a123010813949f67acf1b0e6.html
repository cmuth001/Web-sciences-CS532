Nike Launch the Mercurial Vapor 360

\r\n
\r\n 07 February 2018\r\n
\r\n
\r\n\r\n
\r\n

Nike's next-generation of lo-cut lethal speed comes in the form of the Mercurial Vapor 360 as it drops in to co-pilot the Superfly into a World Cup year of Swoosh styled innovation.

\r\n\r\n \r\n \r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n \r\n \r\n

Nike\'s summer showpiece\xc2\xa0drops in an explosion of Mercurial mayhem as the Swoosh arrive in\xc2\xa02018 town with reloaded weaponry to fire\xc2\xa0up World Cup hype. The Mercurial Vapor sits closer than ever to\xc2\xa0the Superfly as Nike introduce their\xc2\xa0360 degree construction to the upper of the\xc2\xa0iconic speed series. It\'s simply a choice of collar or no collar this time around.

\r\n\r\n\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\r\n \r\n \r\n

That 360 degree construction eliminates any excess space inside the boot by locking your foot in and preventing any slip within the boot. In turn maximising a players top speed. Ribbed Flyknit\xc2\xa0features\xc2\xa0on the upper to provide an added bit of precision touch\xc2\xa0when\xc2\xa0controlling the ball at pace.

Visually, the Mercurial Vapor 360 screams fast with a stunning streamline aesthetic and bold "Total Orange" launch colourway. Re-positioned Swooshes and the introduction of the \'M\' logo on the heel bring refreshed graphic game, but it\'s all about the exquisite soleplate down below.\xc2\xa0

\r\n\r\n\r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n \r\n \r\n \r\n\r\n\r\n \r\n \r\n

The claw-like soleplate is inspired by the Cheetah paw \xe2\x80\x93 an\xc2\xa0aggressive forefoot is built for acceleration in any direction while the heel studs help the player stop on a dime. Fast enough? All Nike players will wear the Mercurial Vapor 360 and\xc2\xa0Mercurial Superfly 360 on-pitch from this weekend onwards.

Pick up the Nike Mercurial Vapor 360 from prodirectsoccer.com\xc2\xa0from Feb 14.
