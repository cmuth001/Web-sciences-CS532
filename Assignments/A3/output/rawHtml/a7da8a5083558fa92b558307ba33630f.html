Who signed where on National Signing Day?

Diana C. Nearhos, USA TODAY NETWORK - Tennessee\n Published 9:51 a.m. ET Feb. 7, 2018 | Updated 7:14 p.m. ET Feb. 7, 2018
CLOSE

\n Signing to play football in college is only the first step. Austin-East coach Jeff Phillips challenges players and parents to make sure they graduate too.\n Diana C. Nearhos, USA TODAY Network - Tennessee

Wednesday marked the beginning of the NCAA signing period for football, soccer and water polo. Here is who signed, or will sign this week, to play in college. This list does not include those who already signed prior to this signing period.

Football

Kevin Andrews, Austin-East – Union College

Jonathan Atchley, King’s Academy – Thomas More College

Jaquez Booker, Fulton – University of the Cumberlands

Logan Bowers, West – East Tennessee State

Tucker Brown, King’s Academy – Thomas More College

Bryson Butsko, Concord Christian – University of the Cumberlands

Kylan Campbell, Austin-East – University of Pikeville
