SEVILLA 2-0 LEGAN\xc3\x89S

Sevilla 2-0 Legan\xc3\xa9s (3-1) Copa del Rey semi-final: goals, report

Joaqu\xc3\xadn Correa and Franco V\xc3\xa1zquez scored at the S\xc3\xa1nchez Pizju\xc3\xa1n to set up a cup final clash with Barcelona or Valencia.

\n

Sevilla maintained their reputation as a remarkable cup team by seeing off Legan\xc3\xa9s at the S\xc3\xa1nchez Pizju\xc3\xa1n to set up a 17th final since 2006. 

\n

Joaqu\xc3\xadn Correa stabbed the ball in to an unguarded net after 15 minutes to open the scoring, following tenacious work by the excellent Luis Muriel.

\n

Legan\xc3\xa9s pushed forward in the second half but suffered from questionable decision making and a lack of quality when they penetrated the Sevilla area. The home side punished them in the closing stages. Substitute Sandro Ram\xc3\xadrez set up Franco V\xc3\xa1zquez on the counter-attack. The Argentine forward surged past Dimitris Siovas and arrowed the ball in to the top corner. 

\n

Los Pepineros are eliminated from the competition they have brightened with their presence. Sevilla will play Barcelona or Valencia in the final. Whatever happens on April 21st, it will be hard to prevent the 2017-18 Copa del Rey remaining in memories as the year Legan\xc3\xa9s entertained, delighted, but fell short.
