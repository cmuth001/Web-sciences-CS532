BOISE STATE FOOTBALL: Broncos Add Three More Players To 2018 Recruiting Class; List Complete With 23 New Players

Posted on February 7, 2018

Boise State completed its new recruiting class Wednesday, adding three players to a group that already included 20 recruits from the early signing period in December. The Broncos’ three new players are quarterback Riley Smith (St. Augustine, Fla.), offensive lineman Dallas Holliday (Sherman Oaks, Calif.) and tight end Tyneil Hopper (Roswell, Ga.).
