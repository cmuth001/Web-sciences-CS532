The Southern Miss football program announced seven new signees on Wednesday with a heavy emphasis on defense.

\n \n \n \n \n

USM head coach Jay Hopson and his staff put a focus on adding depth on that side of the ball, especially on the defensive line. USM announced three defensive linemen.

\n \n \n \n \n

Here\xe2\x80\x99s a breakdown of the USM signees:

\n \n \n \n \n

Shannon Carswell (Freeport, Florida) \xe2\x80\x94 He is one of the bigger high school defensive tackles that Southern Miss has signed in a while. He is listed at 6-foot-4, 295 pounds.

He\xe2\x80\x99s a guy we look forward to coming in and making an impact right away.\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x94 USM defensive line coach Derek Nicholson

\n \n \n \n \n

Nick Dawson (Northeast Oklahoma A&M) \xe2\x80\x94 He is a big defensive end at 6-4, 270. He had 27 tackles and 6.5 sacks last year.

Offer list: Memphis, Arizona, Bowling Green and New Mexico

Coach\xe2\x80\x99s take: \xe2\x80\x9cHe\xe2\x80\x99s a great football player. We look forward to him making an immediate impact for us. An end that\xe2\x80\x99s dynamic and get to get the passer in a variety of different plays. He\xe2\x80\x99s an every down football player. He plays the run just as good as he gets after the quarterback. In my opinion, he\xe2\x80\x99s one of those NFL-caliber players.\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x94 Nicholson

\n \n \n \n \n

T.J. Harvey (Columbus, Georgia) \xe2\x80\x94 The 5-foot-11, 185-pound long-snapper committed to the Golden Eagles in July.

Ky\xe2\x80\x99el Hemby (Iowa Western CC) \xe2\x80\x93 At 6-foot-1, 205 pounds, Hemby will bring nice size at safety. With USM losing its top two corners from last year, Hemby should play immediately with three years of eligibility remaining.

Coach\xe2\x80\x99s take: \xe2\x80\x9cWe\xe2\x80\x99re very excited for him. He\xe2\x80\x99s very smart, very intelligent. Coach\xe2\x80\x99s take: \xe2\x80\x9cHe\xe2\x80\x99s a big time pass rushing specialist. He was definitely a big time get for us. He\xe2\x80\x99s big, he\xe2\x80\x99s tall, he\xe2\x80\x99s fast. Trivenskey Mosley (Shreveport, La) \xe2\x80\x93 USM needed another running back to add to the mix and Mosely is a 5-10, 195-pound runner from Shreveport.

He\xe2\x80\x99s a versatile kid who can catch the ball out of the backfield. \n \n \n \n \n

Malik Shorts (Jefferson Davis County) \xe2\x80\x94 A 5-foot-11, 180-pound defensive back, Shorts maintains USM\xe2\x80\x99s tradition of bringing in speedy players from Bassfield.

\n \n \n \n \n

Offer list: Memphis, Louisiana Tech, Louisiana-Lafayette, South Alabama and Tulane.

\n \n \n \n \n

Coach\xe2\x80\x99s take: \xe2\x80\x9cHe\xe2\x80\x99s definitely a big time player for us. He\xe2\x80\x99s another great player from Bassfield to keep that connection alive. We\xe2\x80\x99re definitely very excited about him. There\xe2\x80\x99s a big opportunity for him to come in. There\xe2\x80\x99s definitely a need in the backend and we can\xe2\x80\x99t wait to work with him.\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x94 Pecoraro.
