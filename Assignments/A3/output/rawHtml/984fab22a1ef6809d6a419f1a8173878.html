Riyad Mahrez has been accused of \xe2\x80\x9ctarnishing his legacy\xe2\x80\x9d at Leicester City as his absence continues following a failed move to Manchester City.

Mahrez is set to miss the trip to Man City this weekend, his third successive Premier League game, and has not trained with the squad since Monday, January 29 ahead of the defeat at Everton.

The Algeria winger is unhappy that Leicester rejected Man City\xe2\x80\x99s late approach during the transfer window, despite the Premier League leaders refusing to match the £80million asking price .

\xe2\x80\x9cIt\xe2\x80\x99s a shame, because two years ago, we saw a Leicester team take this league by storm and he was right at the centre of that,\xe2\x80\x9d said Brighton defender Liam Rosenoir on Sky Sports\xe2\x80\x99 The Debate show .

\xe2\x80\x9cHe was outstanding. When you look back at your career, you look back at your legacy, and his legacy now is tarnished. \xe2\x80\x9cWhen he looks back on this situation in a few years\xe2\x80\x99 time, I think he\xe2\x80\x99ll be disappointed.\xe2\x80\x9d

Leicester are willing to be patient with Mahrez, who was said to be feeling depressed in quotes attributed to a \xe2\x80\x98close friend\xe2\x80\x99 of the player.

Former City midfielder Dennis Wise, who was also on the show alongside Rosenoir and Arsenal legend Ian Wright, believes that Mahrez is letting the club, his team-mates and himself down.

\xe2\x80\x9cI understand his frustration that he wants to go,\xe2\x80\x9d said Wise. \xe2\x80\x9cMan City haven\xe2\x80\x99t matched what Leicester\xe2\x80\x99s price is, which is entirely up to Leicester.

\xe2\x80\x9cYes, you\xe2\x80\x99re not happy about what\xe2\x80\x99s going on and I\xe2\x80\x99m sure, in time, he will end up leaving, but at the moment, he\xe2\x80\x99s letting himself down, he\xe2\x80\x99s letting his team-mates down and he\xe2\x80\x99s letting the club down at this present time.
