    A pair of Tigers take their game to the next level

    \n\t\t

    • Twitter

    \n\t\t

    CHILDERSBURG, Ala. \xe2\x80\x93 Two graduating seniors at Childersburg High School were celebrated Wednesday during football\xe2\x80\x99s famed National Signing Day ceremonies at the school. Takairee \xe2\x80\x9cTK\xe2\x80\x9d Kenebrew and Jelan Kidd signed their scholarship paperwork in front of family, friends, and team mates to take their game to the next level.

    \n

    \n

    Kenebrew (6\xe2\x80\x994\xe2\x80\x9d, 190 lbs.) will play wide receiver for the North Alabama Lions. Kidd (5\xe2\x80\x9910\xe2\x80\x9d, 170 lbs.) will head to Montgomery to play for Faulkner University as a wide receiver.

    \n

    \xe2\x80\x9cI am proud to introduce these youg men,\xe2\x80\x9d said CHS Coach Jonathan Beverly. \xe2\x80\x9cThey are hard workers, good athletes and students, but they are even better people, who do things the right way.\xe2\x80\x9d They took care of business on the field, in the classroom, and on the ACT. I am so proud of them and the example they set for our program.\xe2\x80\x9d

    \n

    Kenebrew will major in Accounting, and Kidd will major in Physical Therapy.
