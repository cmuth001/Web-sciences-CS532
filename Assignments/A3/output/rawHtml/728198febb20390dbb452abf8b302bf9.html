Portland State Vikings add 15 football players on national signing day

\n
\n \n Updated February 7, 2018 at 12:51 PM;\n \n Posted February 7, 2018 at 12:50 PM\n
\n
Highlights of 2018 Football Signee Isaiah Woods
\n\n \n\n \n
\n
\n \n
\n
\n
\n \n
\n \n

\n \n \n By Joel Odom

\n \n \n

jodom@oregonian.com

\n \n \n

The Oregonian/OregonLive

\n \n \n \n \n
\n\n
\n
\n \n
\n

The Portland State Vikings added 15 football players to their roster Wednesday, filling positions of need on national signing day.

\n

Coach Bruce Barnum noted that the Vikings needed to add depth at defensive back, wide receiver and offensive line, the positions where PSU was losing its most seniors.

\n

Two of the new players are transfers from the University of Washington: receiver Isaiah Woods and tight end Brayden Dickey.

\n
